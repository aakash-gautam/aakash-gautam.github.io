NPTEL Course: State-Space Approach to Control System Analysis and Design

By Prof. Akhilanand Pati Tiwari, School of Computing and Electrical Engineering, IIT Mandi
Nature: Introductory Language: English Style: Self-paced Format: Recorded

About Course: This course offers a comprehensive introduction to the state-space approach in control system analysis and design. It is tailored for students who have foundational knowledge in linear systems and aims to equip them with tools to design and analyze control systems using state-space techniques.

About Faculty: Prof. Akhilanand Pati Tiwari is with the School of Computing and Electrical Engineering (SCEE), IIT Mandi. He specializes in control systems and has contributed to both teaching and research in the field. His teaching focuses on providing clear theoretical understanding combined with practical applications.
